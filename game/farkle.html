How to Play

The Goal

Be the first player to reach 10,000 points.

On Your Turn

Roll all 6 dice. You must set aside at least one scoring die to keep rolling. You can then either:

  • Bank: Save your points and end your turn.
  • Roll Again: Roll the remaining dice to add more points.

Farkle! 💥

If you roll and get zero scoring dice, you Farkle. You lose all points gathered during that turn and it's the next player's turn.

Hot Dice 🎲

If you use all 6 dice to score, you have "Hot Dice"! You can pick up all 6 dice and keep rolling to continue your turn.
