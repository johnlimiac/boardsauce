How to Play

The Goal

Be the first player to reach 200 points by flipping cards and banking their values.

On Your Turn

Flip the top card of the deck. You can then:

  • Hit: Flip another card.
  • Stay: Bank your current cards and end your round.

Busting 💥

If you flip a number you already have in your line, you bust! Your score for the round becomes 0.

Flip 7 Bonus 🃏

Collect 7 unique numbers without busting to end the round immediately and get a +15 point bonus!

Action Cards

❄️
Freeze: Forces you to Stay immediately.
🔄
2nd Chance: Discard instead of busting once.

Vengeful Rules

This mode is more aggressive. Even if you Stay, you are still in the round and can be targeted!

Special Cards

0
Zero: Score is 0 unless you Flip 7. Must Hit every turn!
7
Unlucky 7: Discard all cards except this one.
13
Lucky 13: You can have two 13s without busting.

Vengeance Actions

  • Steal: Take a card from another player.
  • Swap: Swap cards between two players.
  • Just One More: Force a player to flip one more card.
