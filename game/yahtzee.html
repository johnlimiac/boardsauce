How to Play

The Goal

Score the most points by rolling five dice to make specific combinations across 13 rounds.

On Your Turn

You get up to 3 rolls per turn:

  • Roll 1: Roll all 5 dice.
  • Roll 2 & 3: Keep any dice you like and reroll the rest.

After your rolls, you must fill one empty category on your scorecard.

Bonuses

  • Yahtzee Bonus: Every extra Yahtzee after the first is worth +100 pts!
